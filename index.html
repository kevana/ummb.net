UMMBNet

Welcome to ummbNet

ummbNet is an event scheduling web app for the University of Minnesota Pep Bands. It allows members of the bands to trade performance slots while giving directors real-time event rosters.

I designed and implemented this webapp from the ground up as part of an independent study course under the direction of Professor Daniel Challou.

It is now archived, visit kevanahlquist.com to see my current projects.
